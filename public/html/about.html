Kappa Theta Pi is a professional fraternity that is heavily invested in technology. At the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology chapter, members must have a major, minor, or extreme interest in computing technology. We hold professional seminars for our members in order for them to network and gain important technical skills. Though we are young, we have a strong sense of brotherhood and host both social and professional events.

At Rose-Hulman, KTP is especially interested in running these types of professional workshops:

  • PLC Scheme Crash Course
  • Soldering Crash Course
  • Entrepreneurship Lectures
  • Talks given by professionals who work in the field
  • Interview Skills Workshop
  • etc

If you think this would look good on your resume and it'd be fun to attend these events with friends, consider rushing Kappa Theta Pi during the winter term!
